Documentation / Overview

The Personal
Statement

One essay. 650 words. Sent to every college on your list. This is the centerpiece of your application.

The Constraints

The Limit

650 words maximum. There is no leeway. The form will cut you off. You must be concise, impactful, and devoid of fluff.

The Goal

This is not a resume. This is a personal narrative. Admissions officers want to know who you are when you aren't in class.

Note: We are ignoring supplemental essays for now. We are focusing strictly on the main Personal Statement. If you master this, the rest is easy.
