Topics vs. Moments

Before you start writing, it is important to know the difference between a topic and a moment. The first mistake students make is that they start too broadly.

The college essay is different from every other essay you've written. Academic essays test what you've learned. The college essay asks you to do something harder: look at your own life and figure out what you actually want to say about it.

This isn't a greatest hits reel. It's not the saddest story you can think of. It's not what you think admissions officers want to hear. It's about discovery-writing to uncover your thoughts, not just reporting what you already know.
