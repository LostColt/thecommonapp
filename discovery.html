Phase 01/Discovery

Topic Mining

The prompt doesn't matter yet. The story matters. We are looking for "small moments" that reveal big character traits.

01

Life Inventory

Set a timer for 15 minutes. List moments, not achievements.

  • • A time you got lost.
  • • A meal you cooked.
  • • A conversation with a stranger.
02

Free Write

Pick one moment. Write for 20 minutes. Don't worry about grammar. Just get the sensory details down.
