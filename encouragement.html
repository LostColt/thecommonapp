The hardest part isn't the writing. It's the honesty.

     

     

The hardest part of this essay isn't the writing. It's the honesty.

You're 17 or 18 years old, and you're being asked to write about yourself with clarity and self-awareness to a room full of strangers who will judge you. That's vulnerable. It's uncomfortable. And it's worth doing well.

Don't perform. Don't write what you think they want. Write what's actually true.

They'll know the difference.
